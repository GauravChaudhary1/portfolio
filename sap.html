Roles and Responsibilities

Developement

Responsible for Product Development
  • Gather the requirements from the customers via portal.
  • Discuss the design and carry out feasibility analysis.
  • Develop and Deploy new features of Product GRC-Process Control.
  • Design class documents for the features in deployment.
  • Quality checks on the solutions delivered via SAP Note.
Responsible for Support and Maintenance
  • Provide an end-to-end support for the issues raised by the customer.
  • Consulting to implement/understand a feature or functionality.
  • Provide a knowledge sharing session on the features to the primary support.
  • Deliver continuos innovations with respect to the product.
  • Monitor the workload and assign the incidents to the team.
  • Continuosly Monitor the global queue to check any escalation.
Responsible for Mentoring
  • Helping a colleague to get through a technical/functional problem.
  • Helping a colleague learn about the process of the product.
  • Explaining the features/functionalities of the product.
